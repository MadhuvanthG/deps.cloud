Helping companies track, monitor, and engage with
project dependencies and dependents
Follow on GitHub Get Started
We provide a set of services that enable automation and analysis of your projects and their dependencies. It works by crawling repositories from GitHub, GitLab, and BitBucket, looking for various dependency management files. We then extract, store, and index dependent library information into a comprehensive graph. This information is then exposed through both gRPC and RESTful endpoints.
